## Throughline

**The throughline of the book is *re-membering*** — the literal putting-back-together of what gets dismembered. Analytical and conceptual thinking slice; that's their function and how complex problems become tractable. But the slicing accumulates a cost: cut off from body, from others, from pre-conceptual contact with what's actually present. The practices the book attends to are a structural counter-process, not a sentimental return.
